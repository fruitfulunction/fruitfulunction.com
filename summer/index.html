Fruitfulunction Summer

This summer is different


Hello Fruitfulunctioners or Fruities, this is a special invitation to a summer celebration! We are glad to see you joining our celebration, and we hope you have a wonderful summer experience with Fruitfulunction! Due to the pandemic last year, we experienced a lot. From lock down to online works, and we saw people who lost their lives because of it. Finally, things has come to an end. Everything is reopening and is going to be great again, but this expereince gave people more ideas. During this celebration, we will give everyone an opportunity to share their experience. We will also host special mini-events during the summer celebration, which is from 6/20 to 7/20!

To join this exciting celebration, we opened up our Discord community for everyone. After joining, find the "Stage" channel in Voice Channels category. We will be hosting fun events during a specific time, and all the information will be available in the server announcement channel. All of the time mentioned is in PDT if not specified. Please convert it to your own time zone in order to keep up with us.

We will also be hosting movie night, and some of them might require a subscription due to legal issues. We will be hosting some movie nights using the third-party software Teleparty/Netflixparty, which is a browser extension that allows people to watch Netflix together and chat together. We strongly recommend you to install it prior to the movie night event, and we will be putting Teleparty links in the announcement channel.

We hope you enjoy this celebration!
